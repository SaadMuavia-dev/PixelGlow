Privacy Policy

PixelGlow Studio ("we," "our," or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data. This policy explains our practices when you visit our website or engage our design services.

What We Collect

We collect only the information necessary to provide our services:

Category Examples Source
Identity Name, business name You, directly
Contact Email address, phone number Contact form, email
Project details Brief documents, brand assets, references You, during onboarding
Technical IP address, browser type, pages visited Automatic (server logs)
Communications Emails, messages sent to us You, directly

We do not collect sensitive personal data (health, financial, or biometric information) and do not run advertising trackers on this site.

How We Use It

Your data is used exclusively to:

  • Respond to enquiries and deliver agreed design services
  • Manage project communication and feedback rounds
  • Issue invoices and process payments through secure third-party processors
  • Send occasional studio updates (only if you opt in; you can unsubscribe at any time)
  • Improve website performance based on anonymised usage data
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

We never use your data for automated decision-making or profiling.

Sharing Your Data

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We share data only in these limited circumstances:

  • Service providers: payment processors (e.g. Stripe or PayPal), cloud storage, and project management tools — all under strict data processing agreements
  • Legal requirement: if required by law, court order, or regulatory authority
  • Business transfer: if PixelGlow Studio is acquired or merges, data transfers under equivalent protections

All third-party providers are assessed for data security compliance before engagement.

You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time. Disabling cookies does not affect your ability to use our core services.

Data Retention

We retain your data only as long as necessary:

  • Project files and communications: 3 years after project completion, for dispute resolution and portfolio reference (with your consent)
  • Invoice and payment records: 7 years, as required by accounting law
  • Enquiry data (no project started): 12 months, then deleted
  • Newsletter subscribers: until you unsubscribe

After the retention period, data is securely deleted or anonymised.

Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you have the right to:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal data we hold about you
  • Correction — request we correct inaccurate data
  • Erasure — request deletion of your data ("right to be forgotten")
  • Portability — receive your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Restriction — request we limit processing of your data
  • Objection — object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw consent — at any time, without affecting prior lawful processing

To exercise any right, email us at privacy@pixelglow.studio. We will respond within 30 days.

Security

We implement appropriate technical and organisational measures to protect your data, including:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data in transit
  • Access control — only authorised team members handle client data
  • Regular backups with encrypted storage
  • Secure deletion protocols when data is no longer needed

No method of internet transmission is 100% secure. While we take every reasonable precaution, we cannot guarantee absolute security. Please notify us immediately if you believe your data has been compromised.

Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for individuals aged 18 and over. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13. If you believe a child has submitted data to us, please contact us immediately and we will delete it promptly.

PixelGlow Studio
