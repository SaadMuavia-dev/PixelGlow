About Me

Where Art Meets the Printed Page

I'm an illustrator, graphic designer, and book designer with over a decade of experience bringing ideas to life on paper and screen. My work lives at the intersection of fine art and commercial craft — from richly detailed editorial illustrations published in international magazines to award-winning book covers that compel readers to pick them up.

I believe that great design is invisible — it pulls you in without announcing itself. Whether I'm setting the typography for a 400-page novel, developing a visual identity for a boutique publisher, or creating an illustration series for a children's book, the guiding principle is always the same: serve the story.
